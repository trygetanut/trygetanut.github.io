# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

## TossUp - Decision Analysis App

### Data We Collect

**Stored on your device only:**
- Your decision questions and analysis
- Decision history and outcomes
- App preferences and settings

**We do not collect or store your data on any server.**

### AI Features

TossUp offers optional AI-powered analysis. If you enable AI:

- **On-Device AI:** Runs entirely on your phone. No data leaves your device.
- **OpenRouter / Bring Your Own Key:** Your decision data is sent to the AI provider you configure. Review their privacy policies:
  - [OpenRouter Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy)
  - [Groq Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/)

You control which AI provider to use, or you can use the app without AI.

### Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

### Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it:
- Uninstall the app, or
- Clear app data in your device settings
